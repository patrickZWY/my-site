What This Is

This page collects the agent guide as a practical architecture-review checklist, plus the source studies that shaped it. The material is strongest for reviewing durable APIs, explicit boundaries, lifecycle rules, error contracts, observability, and risk-aware testing.

My evaluation: it is effective as a design-review lens, but it should be applied with judgment. For small changes, the useful part is the operating rule; for systems, security, storage, concurrency, or compatibility work, the full checklist is worth using.
